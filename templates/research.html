Postraduate Research Project

Flight Dynamics: Perturbations from Non-Spherical Bodies (Asteroids)

Using MATLAB and General Mission Analysis Tool (GMAT) found out the distance after which the perturbations due to the non-spherical shape of the asteroid had no effects on the spacecraft orbiting around the asteroid, that is the asteroid could behave as a spherical body for a two-body system and a three- body system. Project was done underthe guidance of Matteo Cerrioti.
